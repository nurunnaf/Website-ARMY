Profile

BTS (Korean: 방탄소년단; RR: Bangtan Sonyeondan; lit. Bulletproof Boy Scouts), also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed in 2010. The band consists of Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. BTS debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment with the single album 2 Cool 4 Skool. BTS released their first Korean and Japanese-language studio albums, Dark & Wild and Wake Up respectively, in 2014. The group's second Korean studio album, Wings (2016)

Fanbase

ARMY stands for Adorable Representative MC for Youth. ARMY is a fan group of the group from South Korea, BTS or Bangtan Sonyeondan. The group consists of RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook has a large fan group and is spread all over the world. They coined the name "ARMY". The name was launched after BTS made their debut with the single '2 COOL 4 SKOOL'. Precisely on July 9, 2013, BTS held a fancafe in South Korea and announced that their fans would be called ARMY.
